Omi

Origin

Founded in the year 2020, Omi is a music collective with various changeable parts.

Core members include Diji Solanke, Tobias Illingworth and 'unknown drummer'.
This collective are largely responsible for the latest groundbreaking genre known as Acid soul.
They originated in the heart of Newcastle with the majority of the collective, core and extended
having lived on the same street. Sidney Grove

Members

Diji

Adediji Solanke is a Nigerian born multicultural individual (within the confines of his social,geo-political,aesthetic enviroment)
He started learning how to play the guitar at the age of 19 and songwriting followed shortly after.
His main influences include
  • Ani DiFranco
  • Frank ocean
  • Andre 3000
  • Hiatus Kaiyote
To name a few.

Tobias

Tobias Illingworth is a master of music.
He's an improvisationalistic guru who deals in the mystical arts of allthings mo'funkin spiritual
He plays the piano, various stringed instruents. He also sings and does spoken word.
His main influences include
  • Tyler the creator
  • Chaka Khan
  • Frank Zappa
  • Billie Eilish

Unknown Drummer

Unknown Drummer was simply the slickest drummer in the city
He can hold down a groove like no other.
His true genious however, lies in his ability to put the music the group create before his ego. A quality lacking in most.
His mysterious taste in music include the following flavours
  • Unknown T
  • Unknown Motal Orchestra
  • Unknown Brain

The group aim to release and take over the world for a moment in time starting mid July.
Till that time comes. If you're based in the north East of England, keep your eyes and ears peeled.
